The IANA Functions Operator has completed its review of draft-irtf-cfrg-gcmsiv-09. If any part of this review is inaccurate, please let us know.

We understand that this document is requesting a single registry action.

In the AEAD Algorithms registry at

https://www.iana.org/assignments/aead-parameters/

two new algorithms will be registered:

Numeric ID: [ TBD-at-Registration ]
Name: AEAD_AES_128_GCM_SIV
Reference: [ RFC-to-be ]

Numeric ID: [ TBD-at-Registration ]
Name: AEAD_AES_256_GCM_SIV
Reference: [ RFC-to-be ]

One of the three designated experts for AEAD Algorithms has approved these registrations, but they've asked us to wait for the other two experts to confirm before marking the document "IANA OK."

The IANA Functions Operator understands that this is the only action required before publication.

Note:  The actions requested in this document will not be completed until the document has been sent to us and the RFC Editor for processing. This message is meant only to confirm the list of actions that will be performed.

Thank you,

Amanda Baber
