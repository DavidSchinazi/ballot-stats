# Internet AD comments for draft-irtf-nwcrg-tetrys-04
CC @ekline

## Comments

### S5.1.1

* "The length of the whole Header Extension field, ..."

  It might be nice to clarify something like "including the HET and HEL
  bytes" (assuming that is the intended reading).

### S5.4

* "nb_missing_src: the number of missing Source Symbols in the receiver
  since the beginning of the session."

  What happens for an *extremely* long running session?  Is it okay if this
  field wraps around?  Even though is probably highly unlikely, I assume that
  since its purpose is statistical reporting there is no concern here.

## Nits

### S5.1

* "There is 3 types of packets" -> "There are 3 types of packets"
