Technical Summary

  This document describes the Argon2 memory-hard function for password
  hashing and proof-of-work applications.  We provide an implementer-
  oriented description together with sample code and test vectors.  The
  purpose is to simplify adoption of Argon2 for Internet protocols.

  This document is a product of the Crypto Forum Research Group (CFRG)
  in the IRTF.

Working Group Summary

  CFRG history was mostly uneventful. After adopting the document
  it was presented in several face-to-face CFRG meetings.

  Crypto Review Panel reviews were solicited in June 2017.
  2 reviews were provided by Russ Housley and Stanislav Smyshlyaev.
  Comments from these reviews were addressed in -03.

  Following revisions of the draft addressed review comments from
  the document shepherd and various ID-nits.

Document Quality

  There are several implementations of Argon2, including Linux packages.

Personnel

  Alexey Melnikov is the Document Shepherd.
  Colin Perkins is the IRTF Chair.
