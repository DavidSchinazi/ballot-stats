The IESG has received a request from an individual submitter to consider

- 'P-Access-Network-Info ABNF Update'
  as Informational RFC

The IESG plans to make a decision in the next few weeks, and solicits
final comments on this action. Please send substantive comments to the
ietf@ietf.org mailing lists by 2016-02-11. Exceptionally, comments may be

beginning of the Subject line to allow automated sorting.

Abstract


  This document updates RFC 7315, by modifying the extension-access-
  info part of the P-Access-Network-Info header field Augmented Backus-
  Naur Form (ABNF).




The file can be obtained via
https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/draft-holmberg-dispatch-pani-abnf/

IESG discussion can be tracked via
https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/draft-holmberg-dispatch-pani-abnf/ballot/
