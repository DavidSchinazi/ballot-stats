==Purpose==

This document discusses aspects of centralization that relate to
Internet standards efforts.  It argues that while standards bodies
have little ability to prevent many forms of centralization, they can
still make contributions that improve the Internet.

== History==

This draft was initially submitted to the ISE on February 18th, 2023,
having first been considered for publication through the IAB stream.

==Non-IETF Work==

n/a

==Security Considerations==

See Sections 2.1 and 6 of this memo.

==IANA==

The document makes no request for IANA action.

==Reviews==

This draft received reviews from Geoff Huston, Milton Mueller, Richard
Clayton all reviewed the current draft favorably.
