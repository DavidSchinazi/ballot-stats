Abstract


  This document specifies an extension to 6LoWPAN Neighbor Discovery
  (ND) protocol defined in RFC6775 and updated in RFC8505.  The new

  This document specifies an extension to 6LoWPAN Neighbor Discovery

  it protects the owner of an address against address theft and
