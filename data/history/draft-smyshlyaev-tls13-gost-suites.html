draft-smyshlyaev-tls13-gost-suites has been presented to the ISE for
publication as an Informational RFC on the Independent Stream.

==Purpose==

This draft specifies the use of GOST suites in TLS 1.3.

== History==

This draft was presented to the ISE on the 15 May 2022.

==Non-IETF Work==

This work is part of a series of updates to enable interoperable usage
of the Russian national encryption standard.

==Security Considerations==

See Section 9 of the memo.

==IANA==

Previous assignments are redirected to this document for information.
